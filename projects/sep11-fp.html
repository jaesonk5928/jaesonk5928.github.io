SEP 11 Freedom Project

I used Javascript concepts (functions, conditionals, arrays) that I learned from this year & combined it with my tool (Phaser) to make my game.
My game's purpose is to challenge people who are bored & patient. To beat my game, You have to collect "lightning" while timing your jumps to not fall down from the platforms. My MVP & Beyond MVP are:
  • MVP: My MVP product had many errors, such as hitbox inconsistancies (platforms), glitchy lightning particles, and a bugged score
  • Beyond MVP: This mainly was about fixing all previous issues listed in my MVP. I fixed it all & even adding a death/win screen for modern style.
My biggest takeaways during this project were:
  • Use all avaliable resources to guide you. For example, the Phaser documentary helped me get an idea of how Phaser worked & how to apply it in your game
  • Prioritize the bigger components of your project. For example, I had to fix the "glitched" lightning bug first, since it was the biggest feature of my game, rather then fixing the platform's hitbox.
