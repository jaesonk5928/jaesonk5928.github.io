Cursed Sympthonies Movie Project

Genre: Action/Horror

The movie is about a Amateur songwriter Kali Sipos discovers a powerful guitar with a curse, but must ally with a previous victim to free it before falling victim to the curse.

It also has a sequel where Kali Sipos, after stopping the guitar's curse, He must use it to seal a spirit that has past players. He must stop the spirit to become a guitar hero.

Some challenges were that I needed to make it so that there is a link to go back and forth to the actual plot and the sequel. I realized that there is internal links in a website. So I remembered that "a href" is code for internal links.

So after I realized the solution, I copy and pasted the real sequel to be the link and BAM, it worked!

Some Takeaways are that internal links are different from links to external pages + The link to the internal pages had to go on top of the head.
