Year-Long Freedom Project

A year-long project made by Jaeson Kwan. This project was researched for many months now, and I enjoyed it. I had to research about one specific project that I was curious about. I chose business. The reason I chose business was because I was thinking about it for quite a while now, even when I was in elementary school. There were 2 main sections for this project, Part A and Part B . Part A was basically about searching up tools (software/hardware) that your topic uses during the past through present. For example, businesses currently use POS (Point of Sale) Systems, where it helps manage stock, makes transactions easier, etc. Part B is different then Part A. Now, the project wants to know what incoming inventions that people thought of, including ones that are made up by yourself. For example, I came up with a personal buddy who acts like Siri, but helps you do your tasks physically instead of verbally. Overall, I enjoy making this project.
